Blog 1: What I’ve Done So Far

I am Satyam Nahata, a 17 y/o tech and art enthusiast with big dreams.

I’m currently in Grade 12 CBSE — the traditional system sucks, and I hate it. I wish I could’ve used these two years for real self-learning and skill-building. But I’m trying my best to learn important things anyway.

Why I chose this path: I don’t want to study what everyone else is forced to. School never taught us how to communicate, solve real-world problems, or explore our potential. Instead, it crushes us with comparison. I want to rise above that — I’m learning how to manage time, focus on my personal growth, and build my own damn future.

What I’ve Done Till Now

  • Product Management from Product Vidyalaya
  • Effective Leadership – HP Life
  • Nestlé YUP – Idea Generation
  • Currently doing Harvard’s CS50 Web Programming

What’s Next

  • Level up coding, management & communication
  • Join competitions and build public projects
  • Create a great life with purpose, not just grades

Final Thoughts

It’s tough balancing school and personal goals, but I love what I do. This path is full of risk, full of opportunity. Just have faith in yourself — and in God.
